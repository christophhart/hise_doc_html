Module Panel

The Module Panel is the space where you patch together HISE DSP Modules . It always holds the Master Chain , which is the parent element of all Modules that you add to it. Click the plus icon to add a new Sound Generator .

In the signal chain it is also the first and last processed element. Every added Midi Processor preceeds the MIDI Processors of any child and the Gain- and FX Chains are applied to the final sum of all generated sounds at the end.
